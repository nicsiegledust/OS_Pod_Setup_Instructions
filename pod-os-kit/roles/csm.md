# Role Pack: Sales - CSM
Tabs: Today · Accounts · My Plate · Waiting On · Inbox.
System of record: CRM (book of business) + usage warehouse if connected.
work/: `meetings/`, `accounts/`, `renewals/`, `notes/`.
Accounts tab: one row per account: health dot (usage trend + engagement recency), renewal date chip, open risk chip, last-touch age. Same honesty rule: missing data = no chip.
Watch topics (preselected): renewal risk & dates / usage drops & adoption / champion movement (job changes) / open support escalations / meetings & prep / expansion signals.
Engine tweaks: Morning flags accounts silent >N days and usage anomalies; Evening logs touch-points; Weekly produces the book-of-business review (risk-ranked) and upcoming-renewals runway.
Hard rules: outreach drafts only, never sent; escalations create ONE task, not a thread storm.
