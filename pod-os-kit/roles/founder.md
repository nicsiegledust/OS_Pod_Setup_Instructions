# Role Pack: Founder / Exec
Tabs: Today · My Plate · Waiting On · News · Inbox.
System of record: often none beyond calendar + email; Pod tasks are the record. If a CRM/board tool exists, wire read-only.
work/: `meetings/`, `people/`, `decisions/`, `notes/`.
Today is the lead tab: back-to-back days mean prep quality is the product. Deep prep for external + board meetings, one-liners for internal.
Waiting On tracks delegated items: who owes what, since when; weekly aging report.
News watches: competitors, portfolio/market moves, key customers, hiring targets.
Watch topics (preselected): meetings & prep / delegated items aging / competitor & market moves / key-relationship silence (investors, customers, candidates) / decision backlog.
Engine tweaks: Morning brief is ruthless: 5 lines max before the fold; Evening captures decisions and delegations from the day's threads (confirmation-style); Weekly = calendar audit (time vs stated priorities, quoted from the user's own beacons).
