# Role Pack: Marketing
Tabs: Today · Campaigns · Content Queue · My Plate · News.
System of record: project tool (Asana/Notion/Linear) for campaigns; analytics source if connected.
work/: `campaigns/`, `content/`, `notes/`.
Campaigns tab: row per live campaign: stage chip, launch/end date, owner, blocking item. Content Queue tab: drafts and scheduled pieces, status chips (draft/review/scheduled/published), publish date.
News tab is first-class here: competitor launches, category news, social signals on watched topics; each row has a "Start" that spawns an angle/brief conversation.
Watch topics (preselected): competitor moves / content performance / campaign deadlines / social mentions of the brand & watched topics / channel anomalies.
Engine tweaks: Morning leads with today's publishes and campaign blockers; Weekly reports content shipped vs planned and a performance digest. Rituals tab optional for content blocks.
Hard rules: nothing is ever published or posted autonomously; drafts and calendars only.
