# Role Pack: Team OS (shared Pod)
Tabs: Standup · Goals · My Plate (per-member filter) · Waiting On · Inbox.
Differences from a personal OS:
- Members: everyone on the team joins the Pod; capture is shared (items carry `by`).
- Standup tab: yesterday/today/blockers per member, written by the Morning engine from each member's activity (confirmation-style: members correct, not compose).
- Goals tab: the team's weekly goals with owner + binary done state; Weekly engine drafts next week's from the tracker, the lead approves.
- My Plate gains an owner filter chip row; tasks carry an owner field.
- Slack: one team channel; the daily brief tags only people with blockers or decisions.
- Alerts and relay warnings go to the designated MAINTAINER (recorded in STATUS.md), not the whole team.
Watch topics: goal slippage / cross-member blockers / decisions pending > 3 days / external commitments due this week.
Hard rules: the engine never assigns work to a person; it proposes, the lead assigns. Per-member privacy: personal email/calendar data never renders in the shared banner; only member-approved surfaces.
